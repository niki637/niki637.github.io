Black Tea

Black tea is known for its robust flavor and rich color. Popular varieties include Assam, Darjeeling, and Earl Grey. It is fully oxidized and contains more caffeine than other types of tea.

Green Tea

Green tea is unoxidized, which gives it a lighter flavor and higher antioxidant content. It is often associated with health benefits. Famous types include Matcha, Sencha, and Longjing.

Herbal Tea

Herbal teas are made from a variety of plants, fruits, and flowers. They are naturally caffeine-free and include options like chamomile, peppermint, and hibiscus.

Oolong Tea

Oolong tea is partially oxidized, placing it between black and green tea in flavor and color. It is known for its smooth and fragrant profile, with varieties like Tieguanyin and Da Hong Pao.
